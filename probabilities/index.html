Dice Probabilities

How likely was my score?

Enter a final score and a risk preference (θ). The curve is the exact score distribution under optimal play; the shaded area right of your target is the chance of matching or beating it.

How likely is this roll sequence?

Set your first roll, your second roll, and the target you are aiming for. Use the arrows to change a die and click a die to keep it. Each step shows how likely one roll is to reach the next, and their product is the chance of the whole path. A kept die that changes value makes that step impossible (0%).
