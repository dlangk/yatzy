Dice Probabilities

How likely was my score?

Enter a final score and a risk preference (θ). The curve is the exact score distribution under optimal play; the shaded area right of your target is the chance of matching or beating it.

How likely is this reroll?

Set your dice (set 1) and click the ones you keep, then set the outcome you want (set 2). The tool shows the probability of the reroll turning set 1 into set 2. Order does not matter: keeping a value just means the outcome must contain it somewhere, otherwise the reroll is impossible (0%).
